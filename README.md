To bypass the gatekeepers of **Oxford University** and secure a seat in the 2026 BA Jurisprudence intake. 

## 🛡️ CORE MECHANICS
* **Daily Quests:** Non-negotiable tasks (LNAT, Case Law, Personal Statement).
* **The Dragon:** Your AI companion/coach. Ignore him at your peril.
* **Streaks:** Every day of discipline increases the flame. A broken streak resets the progress.
* **Secret Loot:** Completion of all daily tasks unlocks high-tier rewards.

1. **The Admin (Me) holds the keys.** Only the Admin can update the Quest Log and unlock Achievements.
2. **No Skipping Boss Battles.** You cannot claim the reward until every checkbox is ticked.
3. **Potential is the Mana.** Potential is useless if not converted into XP through study.
